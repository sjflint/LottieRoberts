A bit about me

Hi, I'm Nicola Frear - The owner of Lottie Roberts Flowers

As a professional horticulturalist, I’ve worked for the National Trust, the Royal Horticultural Society and the University of York in a career spanning 30 years.

I took a creative leap of faith in 2018 and I’m delighted to have been providing flowers for all occasions ever since.

In early 2019, I became a member of York Wedding Suppliers, a group of talented local businesses providing wedding planning services in York and, as part of collaborative events, I’m delighted that my floral designs have been featured in international publications such as London Life, Hearthome and European Life magazines.

In addition, I regularly exhibit at local wedding fairs at beautiful venues in Yorkshire in collaboration with The UK Wedding Event.
